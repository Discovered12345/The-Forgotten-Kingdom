Major Nubian Kingdoms

Kerma

2500-1500 BCE

One of the earliest urban centers in Africa, known for massive mudbrick structures and the iconic Deffufa temples.

  • Advanced pottery and craftsmanship
  • Extensive trade with Egypt
  • Military rival to Egyptian power

Napata

750-656 BCE

The "Black Pharaohs" who conquered Egypt and ruled as the 25th Dynasty, reviving Egyptian traditions.

  • Piye - First Nubian pharaoh of Egypt
  • Taharqa - Greatest builder-king
  • Revival of pyramid construction

Mero√´

300 BCE-350 CE

The iron-smelting capital of Africa, famous for powerful queens and hundreds of steep pyramids.

  • Advanced iron technology
  • Powerful queen-regents (Kandakes)
  • Unique Meroitic script
